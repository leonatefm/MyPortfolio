    StoredIQ For Legal

    UX Designer & Prototyper, IBM Design

    Organizations today are at a critical intersection of increasing data growth and declining data value, creating a ripple effect across the business and exposing the organization to increasing cost, as well as legal and compliance risk.

    IBM information lifecycle governance solutions help organizations assess and act on data in place to improve their information economics strategy, lower information cost and risk, and maximize data value.

    Benefiting all corporate stakeholders including IT, legal, records and the overall business, IBM provides holistic information lifecycle governance solutions for: defensible disposal, e-discovery, data assessment and cleanup, records and retention, and value-Based archiving, enabling customers to manage enterprise information according to its business value while driving down cost and risk.
